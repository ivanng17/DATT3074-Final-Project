The bars on the top of the visualization and the noise you hear represent the amount of total precipitation experienced within the month. The more precipitation experienced, the longer the bars get and the louder the noise gets.

The bar in the center and the beeping you hear represent the average temperature for that month. The size, colour and direction of the bar and pitch of the beeping changes depending on how hot or cold the temperature is.
